“Educating and Empowering Healthcare Leaders of the Future”

Community Health is a fundamental part of our lives whether we notice it or not. The very basis of society depends on its well-being and protection, most of which comes in the form of Community Health initiatives to uphold high standards of safety. It’s important to recognize that this field, especially in the wake of COVID-19, has taken on an ever changing role in our generation as the first line of defense against unimaginable harms. The Community Health Advocate was established with this in mind. The CHA is dedicated towards educating and empowering healthcare leaders of the future. Everyone deserves the opportunity to serve their community as they see fit, and the CHA wants to build the bridges that connect dedicated students to at-risk populations. Healthcare is more than just medicine or policy, it’s about humanity. The CHA wants to humanize the way we view Community Health policy and provide a platform for students to do so.
